## Project Overview

Securing a sprawling Beverly Hills estate required a gate that offered undeniable security without compromising the property's Mediterranean Revival aesthetic. 

## Fabrication Details

We opted for high-gauge aluminum rather than solid brass to reduce the load on the automation motors, ensuring decades of reliable service without sag. Our finishing team developed a custom multi-stage powder coat procedure that flawlessly mimics the tone and depth of brushed brass, complete with UV-resistant clear coats designed to withstand the harsh California sun.

## Automation & Access

The gate features concealed underground linear actuators, integrated keypad entry, and seamless tie-in with the estate's Control4 smart-home system.

### Technical Specs
*   **Total Width:** 192 inches (16 feet)
*   **Total Height:** 96 inches
*   **Automation:** Dual Underground Motors
*   **Finish:** Custom Faux Brass Powder Coat
