## Project Overview

Securing a sprawling residential estate in Beverly Hills, California, required an architectural entryway that offered undeniable perimeter security while seamlessly complementing the property's elegant Mediterranean Revival aesthetic. The main entrance, positioned at the end of a long, curving palm-lined driveway, demanded a monumental dual-swing gate. 

Working in collaboration with the estate's landscape architect, our team was commissioned to fabricate a 16-foot wide, arched gate. The design called for classical, hand-forged scroll details and the warm, luxurious look of brushed brass, combined with modern smart-home connectivity and high-speed automatic gate operators.

---

## Weight Reduction & Structural Aluminum Engineering

Initially, the client requested that the gate be fabricated entirely from solid architectural brass. However, solid brass is exceptionally heavy, with a 16-foot gate assembly weighing upwards of 2,500 pounds. Hanging that much weight over a wide span creates massive leverage forces on the hinges, causing the gate to sag, bind, and warp over time. 

Additionally, moving such heavy gates puts immense stress on automatic gate motors, leading to frequent mechanical failures. 

To overcome this structural challenge, our engineering team recommended substituting solid brass with **commercial-grade structural aluminum (6061-T6 alloy)**. Aluminum is approximately one-third the weight of brass, yet when properly engineered with internal rib reinforcements and thick-walled tubing, it offers exceptional structural rigidity. By switching to aluminum, we reduced the total gate assembly weight to just **850 pounds**, eliminating any risk of hinge sag and ensuring a smooth, effortless swing that preserves the automatic operators.

---

## Multi-Stage Brass Finishing & UV Protection

To achieve the warm, luxurious luster of brushed brass on an aluminum substrate, our finishing department developed a custom, **multi-stage electrostatic powder coating procedure**. Aluminum does not naturally accept brass chemical patinas, so standard wet paints would look flat and fake. 

Our process began by sandblasting the completed aluminum frame to create a uniform, textured surface for maximum adhesion. We then electrostatically applied a specialized, metallic gold-brass base powder coat and baked it in our industrial curing oven at 400°F. Once cured, our artisans hand-brushed a darker highlighting powder onto the scrolls and pickets, replicating the deep grain of weathered brass. 

The entire gate was then sealed in an architectural-grade, semi-gloss **clear polyurethane powder coat**. This final layer contains advanced UV blockers, protecting the faux brass finish against the harsh, daily California sun and preventing fading, peeling, or chalking.

---

## Smart Automation & Access Control Integration

A modern estate gate must be secure and intelligent. We engineered the gate structure to seamlessly house modern smart-home access systems, pre-routing internal wiring conduits inside the thick-walled aluminum pickets and posts to conceal all cabling. The gate system features:
*   **Concealed Linear Actuators:** Dual underground hydraulic swing operators that are virtually invisible, maintaining the gate's clean, classical lines.
*   **Estate Control Integration:** Seamless wiring into the property's Control4 home automation network, allowing the homeowners to monitor and operate the gate remotely from anywhere in the world.
*   **Integrated Entry Pedestal:** A custom-fabricated, matching brushed-brass intercom pedestal featuring a high-definition video camera and encrypted keypad entry.

---

## Wind-Load & Hinge Bearing Engineering

Driveway gates act as massive sails during high-wind events. To ensure structural stability, we engineered the gate to withstand wind gusts up to **110 MPH**. We fabricated the main support hinge posts from heavy 8" x 8" structural steel columns, clad in matching aluminum sleeves. 

The gates swing on custom, heavy-duty ball-bearing hinges equipped with integrated grease fittings, allowing the 425-pound leaves to be opened or closed with the push of a single finger.
