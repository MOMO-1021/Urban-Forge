## Project Overview

Our clients in Bellevue requested a fireplace screen that offered maximum safety without obstructing the view of their dramatic linear gas fireplace. 

## Fabrication Details

We constructed a seamlessly welded frame from 1/4" solid steel bar and inset a high-gauge woven mesh that provides rigid structure without visual clutter. The entire piece was finished in our signature high-heat blackened patina.
